[About Me

“Every day I want to make a valuable contribution"

Hello! I’m Tertia Pentz a UX Designer based in Sydney. I’m a storyteller and a problem solver; using curiosity, empathy and design skills I bring beauty and ease of use to people’s lives on a day-to-day basis.


As a graphic designer with a background in print and web I've always been frustrated with receiving an interpretation of a third party’s vision of a problem. Through the UX course at General Assembly I discovered a direct way of making a difference to the way people spent their time, leaving my opinion at the door and focusing on the design decision. I am fascinated by how different users interact differently with the same environment or product and would love to focus more on what makes them tick.
