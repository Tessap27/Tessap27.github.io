About Me

The scope of this project involved “linking” two enterprise apps used by NBN technicians to install and maintain the NBN network: Atlas Operate and Atlas Wayfinder

Operate is a more “job” based app focussed on installing a NBN service with specific deadlines and tasks, whereas Wayfinder focus more on general troubleshooting when the NBN network go down.
